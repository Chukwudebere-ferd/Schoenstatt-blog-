About us

Schoenstatt Victorious Youth

Schoenstatt is an Apostolic Movement of Renewal, born in the heart of the Church. Its spiritual center is the Schoenstatt Shrine (Home), where Mary shows Herself as the Mother and Educator who gives birth to christ in us, confirming us according to her image . Among the various branches of schoenstatt movement , the schoenstatt youth is specifically a group of life in the catholic church dedicated to fostering a deep relationship between the young schoenstatt girls and boys who are seeking a closer relationship with the blessed mother and living out a mission of love , service, and faith in our everyday lives.
hence, if you are seeking a community that encourages personal growth, inner transformation, spiritual development, and active participation in activities like the pilgrimages, retreats during lent and advent, summer camps, hangouts, regular group meetings, and other formation programs in creating a better world,
the schoenstatt Victorious youths is for you. the youths are the protagonists of mary initiatives, and at the same time, they are accompanished by counselors and moderators who inspire them to embrace their personal purpose. we therefore invite you to be part of this journey as onr big family - come join us , and lets walk together in faith , hope and love
